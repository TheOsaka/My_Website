Hello there!

I was born on July 16, 1998 in the Melikgazi district of Kayseri.

Since my father was a teacher, I lived in Pınarbaşı until I was 6 years old and completed my kindergarten there.

In 2004, my education life started in Necip Fazıl Kısakürek Primary School, which is my primary school. I completed my primary school process here for the first 5 years and completed my secondary education at 50. Yıl Dedeman Primary School between 2009-2012.

While continuing my school life at Istikbal Anatolian High School between 2012-2015, I completed my secondary education by graduating from Bilim Doruk College in the last year of high school.

I started my business life quickly before I started university and worked as a store manager at ŞOK Marketler for 3 years. Then I started to work as a Call Center Operator at Aselsan. While working here, I had a 6-month break due to my military service.

After my military service (March 2022), I prepared for the university exam and won the Department of Art History (Bachelor) at Erciyes University. But not contented with this, I started my education at a software school (Clarusway) originating from America, thanks to my curiosity and enthusiasm for the software world. Thanks to Clarusway, I continue my studies at full speed due to the increase in my perspective and curiosity about the software world. After graduating as an AWS/DevOps Engineer, I am looking forward to a quick introduction to the software world..
